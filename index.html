A GUIDE FOR YOU - PHILADELPHIA FARMERS MARKET

Farmers Market Locations

Farmers market, a physical marketplace intended to sell food directly by farmers to consumers, is one of the ways to provide fresh, healthy and locally grown food for all. It can not only offer fresher, seasonal, healthier and a better variety of foods, but also serve as a place to meet neighbors and to enjoy an outdoor walk while getting needed groceries. In response to the challenge of ensuring access to healthy food for all, the Philadelphia Food Trust has set up numerous farmers markets throughout Philadelphia.

The map on the right shows you all farmers markets locations in Philadelphia. By clicking the location markers, you can see the name and address of each farmers market, and the bus or subway routes that can take you there.
